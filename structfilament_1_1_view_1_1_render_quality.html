Detailed Description

Structure used to set the quality of the rendering of a View.

This structure offers separate quality settings for different parts of the rendering pipeline:

hdrColorBuffer: sets the quality of the HDR color buffer. A quality of HIGH or ULTRA means using an RGB16F or RGBA16F color buffer. This means colors in the LDR range (0..1) have a 10 bit precision. A quality of LOW or MEDIUM means using an R11G11B10F opaque color buffer or an RGBA16F transparent color buffer. With R11G11B10F colors in the LDR range have a precision of either 6 bits (red and green channels) or 5 bits (blue channel).
